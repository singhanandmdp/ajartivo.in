AJartivo Policies

Privacy Policy

AJartivo collects only the information necessary to operate a secure digital marketplace, manage subscriptions, verify payments, protect downloads, and improve customer support.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as email address and authenticated user ID.
  • Purchase and subscription records required for download access control.
  • Download activity used for entitlement tracking, fraud prevention, and support.
  • Basic device, browser, and IP-related metadata collected for security and service diagnostics.

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate users and secure digital asset delivery.
  • To track free and premium download limits correctly.
  • To verify successful payments and activate premium subscriptions.
  • To investigate fraud, abuse, refund requests, and account issues.
  • To respond to support requests and improve product reliability.

Security

AJartivo does not expose the Supabase service role key to the frontend and does not provide public direct asset URLs for protected files. Access-sensitive operations are processed through secure backend routes.

Third-Party Services

AJartivo may use trusted third-party services such as Supabase for authentication and storage-related data operations, and Razorpay for secure payment processing. Payment card details are handled by the payment provider and are not stored directly by AJartivo.
