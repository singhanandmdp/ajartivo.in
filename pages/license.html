License Information

AJartivo assets are licensed for creative use, not for redistribution of the original source files. The license type depends on the asset listing, your account status, and the usage context.

Personal Use

Suitable for learning, inspiration boards, event mockups, family designs, and private visual projects where the original source files remain under your own control.

Commercial Use

Suitable for client work, campaign visuals, promotional print work, packaging, storefront banners, and edited output for business purposes, provided the original files are not redistributed.

Not Allowed
